I’ve Been Scammed

Follow the steps below to protect yourself and report the scam. Stay vigilant and act quickly.

💳 Protect Your Account

Immediately change your passwords, secure your accounts, and freeze your credit if needed.

Contact your bank as soon as possible. Major Australian banks:

  • Commonwealth Bank: 13 2221
  • ANZ: 13 13 14
  • Westpac: 132 032
  • National Australia Bank (NAB): 13 22 65
  • Macquarie Bank: 1300 551 727

📞 Report the Scam

Contact relevant authorities and use official reporting platforms to lodge your complaint.

Report a Scam

Alternatively, contact Scamwatch: 1300 795 995

📝 Document Everything

Keep records of communications, transactions, and any evidence for authorities.

🔒 Protect Your Devices

Run anti-virus scans, update software, and ensure your devices are secure from malware.
